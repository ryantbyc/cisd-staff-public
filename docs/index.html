This site is under active development. All staff movement data is extracted from official CISD Board of Trustees HR Personnel Reports published in public board meeting agendas.

What this data captures: Professional staff hires (Employment of Professional Personnel) and departures (Personnel Resignations and Retirements) as reported to the Board. Substitute teachers and contract renewals are excluded.

Limitations: Board meeting date is used as the record date. Individual names are not published. Staff headcount is not available from these reports, so turnover rate cannot be calculated.

Numbers may change as historical board packets are re-processed. Use this data for research and trend analysis only.
